About Me

I was born in Delhi, India, and at a very young age, I moved to a different state, Maharashtra. Since childhood, I have had the chance to explore and become part of the diverse cultures within my country. During my time in college, I had the opportunity to meet people from new countries and backgrounds, allowing me to explore their perspectives and ways of living while they could learn more about mine.

I have a background in IT and Media. I have worked as a web developer for Ottawa Therapy Dogs and have worked on Search Engine Optimization for Finnobi Forest. I am currently studing Full-Stack Application Development at NSCC where learning new skills like node.js and server side programming as well as refining my JavaScript and CSS skills.

Over the past few years, I have sold photographs worth a total of 125 USD on Getty images via EyeEm. I have worked on several short films and documentaries, including a feature-length film. I have also covered live events such as concerts and sports.

I have experience in a ton of programs such as Final Cut Pro (certified), Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Blender, Autodesk Maya and the Microsoft Office suite, to name a few.
